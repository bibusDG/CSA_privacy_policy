Combat Shooting Analyzer - privacy policy

PART I – PRIVACY POLICY
1. Introduction
This Privacy Policy explains how Combat Shooting Analyzer (“App”, “we”, “our”, or “us”) handles information when you use our mobile application.
Combat Shooting Analyzer is a performance analysis tool that connects to a dedicated Bluetooth motion device to analyze shooting-related movement and timing data.
We are committed to protecting your privacy and complying with applicable data protection laws, including the General Data Protection Regulation (EU) 2016/679 (“GDPR”).
2. No Personal Data Collection
Combat Shooting Analyzer does not collect, store, transmit, or process personal data.
Specifically:
No account registration is required.
We do not collect names, email addresses, or contact details.
We do not collect location data.
We do not collect device identifiers.
We do not use analytics tools.
We do not use advertising SDKs.
We do not track user behavior.
We do not transmit data to external servers.
3. Motion & Performance Data
The App processes motion and timing data received from the connected Bluetooth gyroscope-based unit.
Important:
All motion and performance data is processed locally on the user’s device.
Data is not transmitted to us.
Data is not stored on external servers.
We do not have access to user-generated training data.
All data remains under the control of the user.
4. Bluetooth Permission
The App uses Bluetooth solely to communicate with the dedicated external motion device.
Bluetooth access is required for core functionality only.
The App does not access unrelated device information.
5. Data Sharing
Because we do not collect or process personal data:
We do not sell data.
We do not share data.
We do not transfer data internationally.
We do not provide data to third parties.
6. Data Retention
Since no personal data is collected or stored by us, there is no data retention by the developer.
Any performance data stored locally on the device can be removed by:
Deleting sessions within the App, or
Uninstalling the App.
7. GDPR Compliance (EU Users)
Under the GDPR, personal data refers to any information relating to an identified or identifiable natural person.
Because Combat Shooting Analyzer does not collect or process personal data:
We are not acting as a Data Controller of user personal data.
No personal data processing takes place.
There is no profiling, automated decision-making, or tracking.
There is no cross-border data transfer.
If you are located in the European Union, you retain full control over any data stored locally on your device.
If you believe any personal data is being processed, you may contact us using the details below.
8. Children’s Privacy
The App does not knowingly collect any personal data from children under the age of 13 (or the applicable minimum age in your jurisdiction).
Since no data is collected, no personal information of minors is processed.
9. Security
All performance data generated during use of the App remains stored locally on the user’s device.
Users are responsible for maintaining the security of their own device, including operating system updates and physical access protection.

Changes will be reflected by updating the “Last updated” date at the top of this document.
Continued use of the App constitutes acceptance of any changes.
